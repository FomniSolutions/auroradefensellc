About Us

Our Mission: At Aurora Defense LLC, our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the tools and knowledge necessary for effective self-defense and responsible firearm ownership.

Company History: Founded in 2023, Aurora Defense has grown from a small operation to a trusted name in the firearms industry. Our founder, Andrew Hoover, envisioned a company that prioritizes quality, safety, and customer education.

Our Team: Our team is composed of experienced professionals. We are passionate about sharing our expertise to help our clients make informed decisions.

Core Values:

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Safety: We prioritize safety in all our products and training programs.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.

Services

  • Firearm Sales: We offer a wide selection of firearms, from handguns to rifles, suitable for personal defense, sport shooting, and hunting. Our knowledgeable staff can help you find the right firearm for your needs.
  • NFA and Silencer Transfers: As a licensed NFA dealer, we provide a comprehensive selection of NFA items, including silencers, short-barreled rifles, short-barreled shotguns, and machine guns. Our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through the entire process—from choosing the right product to assisting with the required paperwork and regulatory compliance.

Expertise and Education
 

We believe that informed customers are responsible customers. Our team is dedicated to educating you on the benefits and legal responsibilities associated with NFA items and silencers. We offer workshops and resources to ensure that you understand the laws, usage, and maintenance of your purchases.

 

 

Customer Commitment
 

At Aurora Defense, we prioritize customer satisfaction. We strive to create a welcoming environment where you can explore our offerings and receive personalized recommendations. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a first-time buyer, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Why Choose Us?

  • Wide Selection: A diverse inventory of firearms and NFA items from top manufacturers.
  • Expert Guidance: Knowledgeable staff with extensive experience in firearms and NFA regulations.
  • Compliance Assurance: Assistance with all necessary documentation and legal requirements.
  • Community Focus: We are passionate about building a responsible firearms community and promoting safe practices.
